A collection of friendly cosmonaut NFTs available on stargaze¶

Each cosmonaut is a 48x48 pixel image with a unique combination of traits. The most important one is its guild defined by the token or special entity the cosmonaut supports. Other features include various backgrounds, different suit and visor designs as well as multiple specials.

Note

This website is a dummy with similar content as planned for the mint on stargaze. Details and all cosmonauts are subject to change. Any feedback is welcome!
